Is All Art Any Good?

Is All Art Any Good?

Chloe Stead:

Creativity is not only for humans. AI-generated artwork has captured the public and even a few collectors' imaginations. But is this truly the future of contemporary art?

Log in and subscribe to receive Art Basel Stories directly in your inbox.

Last fall, Christie's became the first auction house to put an AI-generated artwork under the hammer. Created using a generative adversarial network (GAN) trained on a dataset of 15,000 portraits painted between the 14th and the 20th centuries, the piece sold for $432,500—over 40 times its $10,000 estimate—prompting widespread speculation about whether AI could be behind the next great art movement.

About Us: Where Art Meets Technology

Welcome to Artcritique, a groundbreaking online art gallery dedicated to showcasing the captivating world of Al-generated art. We believe in the trans formative potential of artificial intelligence and its ability to push the boundaries of creativity.

At Artcritique, we curate a diverse collection of artwork created using cutting-edge Al algorithms. These algorithms are trained on massive datasets of images and styles, allowing them to generate stunning visuals that are both innovative and captivating.
